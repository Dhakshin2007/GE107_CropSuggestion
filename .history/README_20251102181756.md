This project, **AI-Enabled Crop Suggestion System**, is developed under
**GE107 - Tinkering Lab (G1 - Monday Afternoon)** at **IIT Ropar**.

The system leverages IoT sensors and AI logic to assist farmers in
selecting the optimal crop for their land based on real-time soil and
environmental data. It uses low-cost hardware, integrates with the
cloud, and offers a user-friendly dashboard for monitoring soil and
climate parameters.
